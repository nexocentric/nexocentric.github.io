A Jam Packed Sunday

Yesterday, I made a decision—I needed a new soundtrack. Not just any music, but something fresh, something electric. So, I sat down at my desk, fired up my software, and started mixing. Twenty minutes in, I realized something: I was starving.

Now, I could have cooked, but why use time for that? Instead, I wanted something better, something that got me moving. So I threw on my jacket, laced up my rollerblades, and headed toward my favorite brunch spot. (Huevos Rancheros > everything.) It wasn’t just about food though. It was a chance to get out, keep up with my training, and enjoy the city without staying cramped indoors.

Halfway there, a track started playing in my head. You know, that deep base that makes you want to move. Before I knew it, I was bustin’ out moves like I had my own private dance floor. A couple people looked my way, probably wondering if I was onto something or just out of my mind lol.

At brunch, I sit back, relax and think about some ideas for my next project. No rush, no agenda—just a moment to enjoy.

They say the world will break your heart ten ways to Sunday. Good thing Sunday comes with brunch.
