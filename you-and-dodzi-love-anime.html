Relationships

I believe that a good relationship helps us grow as individuals, while the wrong choice of partner can negatively impact on every segment of your life. Here is what I have discovered so far about my relationship style and goals.

Monogamy

Monogamy is a non-negotiable point for me. I’m not interested in polyamory in any way, shape or form. My perception of polyamory is that it is a result of commitment issues, fear of missing out and a general avoidance of bonding and responsibility. I am not looking to waste each other's time while you try to convince me otherwise, so please reach out only if you share the conviction that physical intimacy should be reserved solely for one partner.

Communication Style

I am generally a calm and collected person, so I like to communicate calmly without emotional outbursts or arguments. Sometimes this means taking a step back to cool down and returning to the conversation with a solution-based mindset. One of the most common pitfalls of relationships is a lack of honesty (with each other, but also with ourselves), so I find it is very important to be clear about your needs and expectations and to avoid making assumptions that ultimately lead to disappointment. The truth is not always pleasant, but I truly value honestly and constructive dialogue.
